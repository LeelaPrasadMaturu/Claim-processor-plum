How It Works

1

Document Verification

Validates uploaded documents match claim requirements. Catches wrong or unreadable documents early with specific error messages.

2

Data Extraction

Uses AI vision to extract patient details, diagnosis, treatment, and amounts from prescriptions, bills, and reports.

3

Policy Validation

Checks waiting periods, exclusions, sub-limits, pre-authorization requirements, and applies network discounts.

4

Fraud Detection

Identifies suspicious patterns like multiple same-day claims, high-value submissions, and document inconsistencies.

5

Decision Engine

Combines all agent outputs to produce APPROVED, PARTIAL, REJECTED, or MANUAL_REVIEW with full explainability.

✓

Full Trace

Every decision includes a complete audit trail showing what was checked, what passed, and why the decision was made.
